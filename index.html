🧮 数学探索挑战 - 台风天 (Math Exploration Challenge) for Typhoon Day ▼

📝 问题 A (Question A)

选择任意一个整数。Select any integer.

当你将这个数字两边的数字相乘时会发生什么？What happens when you multiply this number by the number on its left and right?

例如，如果你选择7，计算6×8。重复几次。For example, if you choose 7, calculate 6×8. Repeat a few times.

注意到什么有趣的地方吗？说服自己这总是会发生。Notice that something interesting happens? Convince yourself that this always happens.

📝 问题 B (Question B)

写出三个连续的数字，其中没有一个是3的倍数。如果你做不到，请解释为什么。Write three consecutive numbers that none of them is a multiple of 3. If you can't do it, explain why.

📝 问题 C (Question C)

选择120的两个互质因子（它们的最大公因数为1）。Select two coprime factors of 120 (their greatest common divisor is 1).

将它们相乘并记录结果。Repeat a few times.

注意到你的结果有什么特点吗？Notice that something interesting happens? Convince yourself that this always happens.

从120以外的数字开始。同样的情况总是发生吗？Convince yourself that this always happens.

📝 问题 D (Question D)

选择任意两个连续的偶数。Select any two consecutive even numbers.

将它们相乘并记录结果。Repeat a few times.

注意到什么有趣的地方吗？Notice that something interesting happens? Convince yourself that this always happens.

🎯 最终挑战 (FINAL CHALLENGE)

取任意一个大于3的质数，将其平方并减去1。重复几次。Take any prime number greater than 3, square it, and subtract 1. Repeat a few times.

注意到什么有趣的地方吗？Notice that something interesting happens? Convince yourself that this always happens.
